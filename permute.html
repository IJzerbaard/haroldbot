haroldbot 2 (permutation mode)

Examples

Rotate by 32 (rotations are not detected)
    32..63 0..31

Simple bit-swap
    1 0 2..63

Reverse bits
    63..0

Large permutation with don't-cares
    3 11 19 27 * * * 2 10 18 26 34 * * 1 9 17 25 33 41 * 0 8 16 24 32 40 48 * 7 …

Notes

This is (for now) only a tool to generate permutations of a 64bit word using delta swaps with power-of-two shift counts, with the large shift counts at the begin and end and the shift count of 1 in the middle. Steps with a mask of zero are omitted, in the worst case an 11-step Beneš network will be used. As special cases the result may be a butterfly network or a single delta swap or even nothing. No other approaches are attempted so in general the result is not optimal.

The solving approach is pretty dumb, I just make a routing network with variable masks, constrain the output indices, and have MiniSAT solve for the masks.

Since everything happens client-side, I don't get automatic bug reports. Please open an issue or contact me (eg via email or twitter @HaroldAptroot).
